Goodbye Almost Lover

Letting go is always the hardest part.

Posted by Quakelyn C. Lisayen on January 28, 2016

If I'm not mistaken, its been two years and seven months since my sister introduced us with each other. Inever like you in the beginning 'cause you're younger, weird and I hate the way you laugh!

But life is funny coz as time passed by, I started liking everything abou t you and I found the real happiness that's been missing for a long time. Maybe because its been three years that I've been single so I was easily hooked by the emotions. I was very eager, aggressive and fast which made you distance yourself from me. I was stupidly in love? I dunno!

After two months of too much effort for you, I realized something about me. It was not the same me anymore. I can't keep my cool and I suddenly got mad at myself tot he point that I never wanted to see you.

Last year, I swallowed my pride and messaged you coz you're one of the godparents of my nephew. After that, I kept seeing you around lately so I was being friendly until people kept on teasing me. At first I always tell myself that "past is past" and I will not like you again. BUT I was wrong, your smile kills me!

My sister and some friends told me to take it slowly this time and cherish the friendship. We were all good since Christmas but suddenly you were cold. A girl kept on telling things about you everytime I'm near her so I texted you that I heard a rumor about you but you don't wanna talk about it.

I will always be ther for you

Before those, we had planned watching a movie together coz of the pustahan LOL! But now, will it just be a plan? I thought were all good now? I know were just friends but you won't let me help you so I don't know what to do.

What if I never left you before? Will there be a difference?
Maybe, we met again to fix what happened in the past and to be good friends. Well, I'm just a call away.

To my bestfriends who will read this, I know your reaction already.
